# Design Doc: Track-App Promotional Video (9:16)

## Overview
A dynamic vertical video for TikTok/Reels/Shorts targeted at dropshippers. The goal is to show how Track-App solves the problem of "shipping transparency" by giving the merchant full control over tracking status and location.

## Target Audience
- Dropshippers
- E-commerce business owners

## Visual Style
- **Format**: 1080x1920 (9:16)
- **Duration**: 15 seconds
- **Vibe**: High-energy, tech-focused, professional UI

## Script / Storyboard

### Scene 1: The Pain (00:00 - 03:00)
- **Visuals**: Fast-paced montage of "angry" message notifications overlaying a dark red background.
- **Text**: "Dropshipping nightmare?", "Stop losing customers."
- **Audio**: Sound of multiple notification pings, fast-paced background music starts.

### Scene 2: The Solution (03:00 - 05:00)
- **Visuals**: Clean transition to the Track-App logo on a professional white/light-blue background.
- **Text**: "Take Full Control."
- **Audio**: Music intensifies (bass drop).

### Scene 3: The Dashboard (05:00 - 10:00)
- **Visuals**: Simplified Admin Dashboard UI. 
  - Click on "New Track".
  - Type Tracking Number: `TRACK-2026-X`.
  - Animate the status slider from `Pending` -> `In Transit` -> `Delivered`.
  - Map pulses and shows a smooth route line.
- **Text**: "Create and manage tracks in seconds."

### Scene 4: The Result (10:00 - 13:00)
- **Visuals**: Customer-facing tracking page.
  - Big "Delivered" or "Arriving Today" status.
  - Professional map visualization.
- **Text**: "Build trust. Scale faster."

### Scene 5: Outro (13:00 - 15:00)
- **Visuals**: Logo, tagline, and call to action button.
- **Text**: "Track-App. Get Started Now."

## Technical Stack (HyperFrames)
- **Runtime**: HTML/CSS/JS
- **Animations**: GSAP (Timeline-based)
- **Assets**: 
  - Custom SVG icons for status
  - App screenshots or simulated UI components
  - Background music (MP3)
